About

Hi! I'm Mira. I make zines, draw little pamphlets about fungi and tide pools, and run a slow-shipping online shop from a stone cottage somewhere mossy. This website is my front door — neither business card nor portfolio in the corporate sense. More like the wreath you hang up to say "we're open today."

Most days I'm drawing. Some days I'm writing about field trips, kitchen experiments, or whatever the cat got into. I post a new zine roughly every six weeks. The webring in the corner is real and active — there are 47 of us now, slowly cross-pollinating.

What this site is not

It's not optimized for conversions. There is no popup, no cookie banner (the site collects nothing). It will never use AI-generated illustration as final output. The hosting bill is paid by a small Patreon and goodwill.

If you came here looking for a graphic designer for hire: I'm not available right now, but you might enjoy the work of Aria Voss, who builds Frutiger Aero portfolios for clients with serious intent.
