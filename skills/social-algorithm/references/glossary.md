# Glossary

Key terms from the organic personal brand system + IG algorithm archive.

**Topic-centric algorithm** — current distribution model: IG shows content by interest, not by follow (Ch 2, 7)

**Profile-centric algorithm** — old pre-shift model: followers guaranteed visibility (Ch 2, 7)

**Acts 1-4 (4H Course)** — structure of the full system: context, content, marketing, integration (Ch 4)

**Bio anatomy** — the 3-4 line structure of a converting bio: clarity+specificity / differentiation / authority / optional CTA (Ch 4)

**"Kill the verb help"** — bio rule: drop "I help…" (makes you identical to all competitors); replace with imperative, first-person verb, "They call me when…", or "You find me when…" (Ch 4)

**"Would I give this person my money?" test** — 1-10 gut check applied to a bio to judge whether it justifies a purchase (Ch 4)

**Bio link (Linktree problem)** — max 3 / ideally 2 links (a free resource + a direct-contact funnel); the profile is itself the landing page, so remove options (Ch 4)

**Lasting asset** — the personal brand seen as a long-term investment, not a tactical lead gen (Ch 4)

**B-roll** — clips of "you doing things" intercut with the speech, never stock (Ch 5)

**Need (principle of)** — one of the 4 elements of indirect selling; perceived gap between the current and desired state (Ch 1, 6)

**Carousel (IG format, revival)** — format rewarded by the 3 current pillar metrics, especially with audio (enters the Reels tab) (Ch 7)

**Hero's journey** — the third element of indirect selling; documenting values / journey, not a heroic tale (Ch 1)

**Complementary channels** — owned (school, newsletter) + non-owned (YouTube, LinkedIn, TikTok, Substack) (Ch 2)

**Capturing attention** — the real goal today: turning views into action, not collecting views (Ch 2, 5)

**Compound effect** — content that keeps generating value over time (especially YouTube) (Ch 3)

**Shares** — one of the 3 pillar IG metrics (Ch 7)

**Face ↔ domain connection** — speed at which the prospect associates you with your service (Ch 6)

**Attraction content** — useful, practical, "interesting profile"; structure different from sales (Ch 6)

**Sales content** — creates gap / need, structured to trigger action (Ch 1, 6)

**CTA (Call To Action)** — the last 5% of the process; without the 4 elements upstream it doesn't work (Ch 1)

**Creative director** — microteam role handling content research / direction (Ch 3)

**Distribution by interest** — first epochal algorithm change (Ch 2)

**Documenting the journey** — modus operandi of creating content from your real week (Ch 1)

**Direct experience** — the first element of indirect selling; basing communication on your lived life, not just "how to" (Ch 1, 5)

**Dopaminergic experience** — passive use of social media without intent (Ch 2)

**Flexing** — anti-pattern: bragging personal results instead of showing client results (Ch 1)

**Off-feed loyalty** — current strategy: Stories + email + private communities (Ch 2)

**Trust (the new Bitcoin)** — the scarcest and most valuable asset in the market (Ch 4)

**Full day blocking** — practice: blocking ONE day per month on the calendar to record everything, with months of lead time (Ch 3)

**Ethical funnel** — mechanism that breaks comfort-zone inertia without being pushy (Ch 6)

**Gap (current vs desired situation)** — the only real purchase trigger (Ch 6)

**Editor (microteam)** — microteam role: video editor (Ch 3)

**Inside Out (method)** — content ideation through journaling + extraction from client calls (Ch 3)

**Knowledge commodity** — after AI, informing has value close to zero (Ch 6)

**Lead magnet** — free resource to bring followers onto an email list (Ch 2)

**Likes (devaluation)** — no longer a driver of the IG algorithm (Ch 7)

**Pinned manifesto** — content fixed at the top expressing values / approach = automatic filter (Ch 1)

**Sophisticated market** — distrustful audience no longer responding to overused direct CTAs (Ch 1)

**Market sophistication (5 stages)** — how many times the market has already heard a promise like yours: plain claim → amplified claim → mechanism → amplified mechanism → identification/you. Saturated market = a generic promise won't enter no matter how good the content (Ch 4, 6)

**Competitive density** — under topic-centric distribution you compete with EVERYONE covering the interest, not just the 3 rivals your followers also follow (Ch 2)

**Contrarian opening** — hook that opens with an unexpected line dismantling a common myth, instead of the generic "X tips for Y" (Ch 2)

**Directional content** — content aimed at the person you want to help (inside the interest bubble / purchase window), as opposed to chasing views/trends; exception = mass markets where volume is the play (Ch 2)

**Non-linear flow** — the dead `content → inquiry` path replaced by 1-3 touchpoints forming an ecosystem (Ch 4)

**Touchpoints (1-3)** — multiple contact points on the account (lead magnet, quiz, bio link, Stories, reactivation) so prospects can enter the orbit several ways (Ch 4)

**Proactive DMs** — opening conversations yourself without the expectation of forcing a call; warms prospects and yields market data (Ch 4)

**Visual metaphor** — dynamic video technique; especially useful for abstract niches (Ch 5)

**Creative microteam** — delegation model that keeps the "personal": director + support + editor (Ch 3)

**Author mode (Ken Follett)** — scripting model based on multiple drafts (Ch 3)

**One-man-show** — stage 1 of delegation: you do it all (Ch 3)

**Recording support** — microteam role that assists you during video sessions (Ch 3)

**Organic (current definition)** — inquiries and revenue without ADV budget, possibly complemented with minimal ADV (Ch 4)

**Personal payoff** — recurring key phrase in your material (e.g. "things don't work, you make them work") (Ch 1)

**Pillar (method)** — content ideation by breaking macro → micro down to the "single piece" unit (Ch 3)

**Positioning** — primary multiplicative lever; verticalization = scalability (Ch 6)

**Reference (ideation method)** — targeted scrolling + "Interested" collections on a dedicated account (Ch 3)

**Reference Based (scripting)** — using the skeletal structure from a reference video via Turbo Scribe (Ch 3, 5)

**Revenue / Hours Invested** — the correct formula for evaluating content ROI (Ch 3)

**Saves** — one of the 3 pillar IG metrics; "this profile interests me" signal (Ch 7)

**Screening** — content that pushes the misaligned away; sign of good positioning (Ch 1)

**Trailer-style script** — short sentences, jagged, frequent line breaks (Ch 5)

**Minimum home setup** — light + tripod + background + decent phone (Ch 3, 5)

**Organic system** — method to acquire clients without ADV (Ch 4)

**Social proof as garnish** — the fourth element of indirect selling; testimonials everywhere, never standalone (Ch 1)

**Stories** — the only remaining outpost for the relationship with a loyal community (Ch 2)

**Dead talking head** — anti-pattern: static talking video in front of a wall (Ch 5)

**Time on profile** — devalued KPI after the algorithm shift (Ch 2)

**Try-and-Real** — IG feature for testing content on new audiences (Ch 2, 7)

**Turbo Scribe** — video transcription tool used to extract skeletal structure (Ch 3, 5)

**Indirect selling** — communication structured on 4 elements; opposite of tactical CTA (Ch 1, 6)

**Verticalization** — narrowing target / domain for clear positioning (Ch 6)

**Watch time** — one of the 3 pillar IG metrics (Ch 7)

**Diagnostic matrix** — "lots of X but few Y" framework to diagnose performance gaps and apply a targeted fix (Ch 7)

**Repost (IG feature, Aug 2025)** — redistribution of your own post in the feed as a new post, bypassing topic-centric distribution toward followers (Ch 7)

**Discovery-first algorithm** — current model showing content to non-followers more than to followers; makes the repost necessary (Ch 7)

**Non-inflated hook** — a specific hook that replaces clichéd formulas ("Tips for X", "How I did Y") with concrete framing (Ch 7)

**Active social listening** — listening to the community + replying with a coherent tone; brand-building lever (Ch 7)

**Mascot / recognizable face** — identity lever not replicable by AI or competitors; substitutable with your face if you do not have a mascot (Ch 7)

**Long-form explainers (format)** — text-heavy / explanation-rich content that rewards watch time (Ch 7)

**Community Notes (Meta, Jan 2025)** — user-based moderation system that replaced professional fact-checking (Ch 7)

**RedNote (Xiaohongshu)** — Chinese social network that became the alternative to TikTok USA after the January 2025 ban threat (Ch 7)

**I See You / I Validate You / I Associate You** — personal brand triangle framework: the mental process the prospect has to go through to remember you and buy (Ch 4)

**Ethos** — character, values, principles guiding your work; first sub-element of "I See You" (Ch 4)

**Recurrence** — elements / phrases / colors / styles that stick to you (same t-shirt, signature format, payoffs) (Ch 4)

**Personal storytelling** — documenting context + journey + ups and downs over time, not a heroic tale (Ch 4)

**4 Circles of Hell** — personal brand anti-patterns: wing-it navigators, fishermen's clique, pot sellers, dock laborers (Ch 4)

**Wing-it navigators** — they make content with no method, waiting for clients (Ch 4)

**Pot sellers** — every post = CTA or pitch, they burn interactions and the community (Ch 4)

**Dock laborers** — they post + run ADV on non-existent structure (Ch 4)

**Purchase window** — the moment the target becomes ready to buy (usually: realizes they can't do it on their own OR has tried without success) (Ch 4)

**5 Awareness Levels** — unaware / problem-aware / solution-aware / product-aware / most-aware (Ch 4)

**Purchase process** — the sequence of steps the target takes before buying; varies by niche (Ch 4)

**Profile funnel** — the IG profile itself as a conversion funnel (bio + pinned posts + bio link) (Ch 4)

**Story funnel** — a Stories set structured to drive to a conversation / call (Ch 4)

**Lead magnet funnel** — free resource + keyword + ManyChat to generate conversations (Ch 4)

**Quiz funnel** — typeform + personalized results to qualify leads (Ch 4)

**ManyChat** — Instagram DM automation tool (Ch 4)

**Double confirmation** — ManyChat best practice: never send a resource without the user clicking twice (Ch 4)

**Trial Reels / Try-and-Real** — IG feature to test content on new audiences, a long shot on volume (Ch 4)

**Indirect Stories** — Stories without an explicit pitch (behind the scenes, reviews, client cases, stances) (Ch 4)

**Direct Stories / Sales Set** — structured sequence: gap hook + elements + closing slide + CTA (Ch 4)

**Proactive-reactive spectrum** — balance between active actions (follower outreach) and passive ones (waiting for them to write); smaller / saturated = more proactive (Ch 4)

**Follower outreach** — proactive funnel: you DM after they follow you, give-first (Ch 4)

**Chat reactivation** — chats don't end, they are picked up after hours / days / months (Ch 4)

**Prospecting (ADV)** — ad campaigns on already validated content to bring new traffic to funnels (Ch 4)

**Retargeting (ADV)** — campaigns on those who have interacted in the last X months (Ch 4)
