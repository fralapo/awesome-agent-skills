## Cross-Cut Observations

Observing across these 12 prompts:

1. **Every prompt ≥ 10s uses 3 timecoded shots.** Never 2, rarely 4.
2. **Micro-detail clauses compound per-line** (see D, E, F). 5–8 details is the zone.
3. **Dialogue cues are short + directional** — pauses specified in ms.
4. **Multi-modal chains max out at 4 refs** (see I, K). Each ref serves one role.
5. **Negative prompts are CN-idiomatic** — English prompts skip them more often, but they help.
6. **Director/film refs compress 10 descriptive lines into 2 words** — highest leverage tokens.
7. **Vertical 9:16 prompts explicitly declare** `画面比例为9:16` or `Vertical Format`. Default is 16:9.
